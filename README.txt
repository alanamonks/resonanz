DEPENDENCIES

- SDL2 and SDL2_ttf, SDL2_image, SDL2_gfx (NOT SDL1.2 or something similar)
  You need to fetch these and compile them from libsdl.org and elsewhere

SDL2: libx11-dev libxext-dev
SDL2_image: libtiff-dev
SDL2_ttf: libfreetype6-dev
SDL2_mixer: SMPEG (svn co), libflac-dev, libvorbis-dev, libfluidsynth-dev (MIDI)
